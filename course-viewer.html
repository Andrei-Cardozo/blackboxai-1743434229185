Ethical AI Fundamentals

1. Introduction to AI Ethics

Artificial Intelligence is transforming our world, but with great power comes great responsibility. This module explores:

  • The fundamental principles of ethical AI
  • Historical context of AI ethics
  • Key ethical frameworks
  • Real-world case studies

Core Principles

Transparency

AI systems should be explainable and their decision-making processes understandable to users.

Accountability

Clear responsibility must be established for AI system outcomes and decisions.

Case Study: Algorithmic Bias

A 2023 study revealed significant racial bias in facial recognition systems:

  • Error rates up to 35% higher for darker-skinned women
  • Only 1% accuracy improvement since 2018
  • Industry-wide standards still lacking

Key Takeaway

Ethical AI requires diverse development teams and rigorous testing across demographic groups.
